About Me

My introduction

Hello, I am Young Eun Jeon, a data scientist interested in resolving various real-world problems through statistical and machine learning techniques.
My focus lies not only on statistical inference but also on developing predictive models with superior performance by combining statistical and machine learning methods.
The primary goal of my research is to transform data into a core tool for practical decision-making, driving meaningful social change in the process .
I aim to provide innovative and practical solutions to real-world problems by proposing new methodologies in the field of data science, making significant contributions across academia and industry.
I am actively exploring opportunities in the academic and professional job market!
